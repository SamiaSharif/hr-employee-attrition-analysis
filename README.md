# HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition patterns using HR workforce data. The objective is to identify the major factors contributing to employee turnover and provide actionable recommendations to improve retention.

## Business Problem
The company is facing employee attrition and wants to understand the root causes behind employee turnover.

## Key Questions Answered
- What is the overall attrition rate?
- Which departments have the highest attrition?
- Is lower salary linked with attrition?
- Are younger employees more likely to leave?
- Does lower tenure increase attrition?
- Does attrition vary by gender?
- What are the key drivers of attrition?

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
- Lower salary employees showed higher attrition.
- Younger employees were more likely to leave.
- Employees with fewer years at company had higher attrition.
- Sales department showed the highest attrition.
- Gender did not show significant impact on attrition.

## Recommendations
- Improve onboarding for early-career employees
- Review entry-level compensation
- Create growth paths and promotion opportunities
- Address department-specific issues
- Build retention dashboards
